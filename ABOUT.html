OUR LEGACY

Empowering women through technical education for over 60 years.

History of PCPS

Founded in **1964** in the heart of Guwahati, P.C.P.S. Girls' Polytechnic was established with a revolutionary vision: to provide technical skills to women in a time when engineering was male-dominated. Over the decades, we have evolved from a small institute into a premier **Government Polytechnic** under the Directorate of Technical Education, Assam.

The institution has survived the test of time, consistently producing rank-holders and industry leaders who now serve in top government and private sectors globally.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, fostering innovation and self-reliance among women to lead the global technological landscape.

Our Mission

To provide quality skill-based training, industry exposure, and ethical values that prepare our students for immediate professional success.
